Congratulations on finding that special one to spend your life with, we wish you and your spouse a fantastic married life ahead. As per Indian weddings tradition, choosing the right Saya/Shubh Muhurat/wedding date for wedding, engagement, roka and other Indian wedding ceremonies is very important. We are sure your loved ones; brother, sisters, uncles, aunties, Bua, Masi, friends,cousins, and all the other close relatives must be busy preparing their shopping list. What to wear on particular sagan, engagement & wedding ceremonies, and choosing the best wedding songs that they want to dance on, what would be the venue and how will it be decorated, what will be the menu in the wedding; after all, this is the best part of a fat Indian wedding. But how will all this wedding planning for Indian style wedding happen without knowing the shubh vivah muhurat? While the best dates might vary with your date of birth, destination of wedding, spouse’s birthday etc, we have identified the most pious dates/saya/shubh muhurat dates for your roka, engagement, sagan and wedding ceremony in 2019. We know wedding planning and finalization of the best destination wedding, wedding venue, wedding decorator, DJ, wedding jewelry & attire is tough and needs planning, we hope these dates give you some help in deciding your special day.
